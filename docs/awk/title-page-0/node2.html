Standard Vector and Matrix Handling

CMTNUL
- Initialises a complex matrix to zero
CVCNUL
- Initialises a complex vector to zero
DYAD
- Forms the dyad of two vectors
MATADD
- Adds two full matrices together
MATCOP
- Copies one full matrix into another
MATIDN
- Initialises a matrix to the identity matrix
MATINV
- Inverts a full matrix (for limited sizes)
MATMUL
- Multiplies two matrices together
MATNUL
- Initialises a full matrix to zeros
MATRAN
- Forms the transpose of a full matrix
MATSUB
- Subtracts one full matrix from another
MATVEC
- Multiplies a full matrix by a vector
MVSYB
- Multiplies a symmetric band matrix by a vector
MVUSB
- Multiplies a unsymmetric band matrix by a vector
SCAPRD
- Forms the scalar product of two vectors
VECADD
- Adds two vectors together
VECCOP
- Copies one vector into another
VECMAT
- Pre-multiplies a full matrix by a vector
VECNUL
- Initialises a vector to zeros
VECSUB
- Subtracts one vector from another
VMSYB
- Pre-multiplies a symmetric band matrix by a vector
VMUSB
- Pre-multiplies an unsymmetric band matrix by a vector
